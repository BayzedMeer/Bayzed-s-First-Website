Bangladesh has decided to extend the nationwide lockdown for yet another week to counter the
spike in coronavirus infections, an official said on Monday.

“The restrictions will continue for one more week from Thursday,” the government’s Chief
Information Officer Surodh Kumar Sarker told reporters, referring to a senior officials’ meeting
that reviewed the latest COVID-19 situation.

19 April 2021, Condition of Covid 19 pendamic in Bangladesh :

  1. Bangladesh has recorded over 100 Covid-19 single-day deaths for the third consecutive day
  2. On Sunday, another 102 deaths were logged in 24 hours.
    • Sixty-eight of the deceased died in Dhaka
    • 22 in Chittagong, four each in Mymensingh and Barisal, three in Rajshahi, and one in Khulna
  3. The day’s infection rate stands at 19% while the overall rate is 13.9% in the country.
